Hymns, #1062

Gospel - Trusting the Lord

Eng/Kor:1062  Chin:727  Span:-  Rus:548  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:William McDonald
Music:William G. Fischer
Meter:7.7.7.7. with chorus.
Guitar:Capo 3, F Major [Sheet Music]

Hymn using same tune: #785 (Tune 2)

1057 1058 1059 1060 1061 More 1063 1064 1065 1066 1067

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4 

 

 1 

I am coming to the cross;

I am poor, and weak, and blind;

I am counting all but dross;

I shall full salvation find.

 

I am trusting, Lord, in Thee,

Blessed Lamb of Calvary;

Humbly at Thy cross I bow,

Save me, Jesus, save me now.

 

 2 

Long my heart has sighed for Thee;

Long has evil dwelt within;

Jesus sweetly speaks to me,

"I will cleanse you from all sin."

 

I am trusting, Lord, in Thee,

Blessed Lamb of Calvary;

Humbly at Thy cross I bow,

Save me, Jesus, save me now.

 

 3 

Here I give my all to Thee —

Friends and time and earthly store,

Soul and body Thine to be —

Wholly Thine forevermore.

 

I am trusting, Lord, in Thee,

Blessed Lamb of Calvary;

Humbly at Thy cross I bow,

Save me, Jesus, save me now.

 

 4 

In the promises I trust;

Now I feel the blood applied;

I am prostrate in the dust;

I with Christ am crucified.

 

I am trusting, Lord, in Thee,

Blessed Lamb of Calvary;

Humbly at Thy cross I bow,

Save me, Jesus, save me now.
