Hymns, #299

Assurance and Joy of Salvation - Reconciled to God

Eng/Kor:299  Chin:240[*]  Span:143  Rus:61  Tag:299  Fra:299  Por:159

Lyrics:C. Paget
Music:Hugh Wilson
Meter:8.6.8.6.
Guitar:No Capo, G Major [Sheet Music]

Hymns using same tune:

#104, #349

294 295 296 297 298 More 300 301 302 303 304

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4   5 

 

 1 

A mind at perfect peace with God;

   O what a word is this!

A sinner reconciled through blood;

   This, this indeed is peace.

 

 2 

By nature and by practice far,

   How very far from God,

Yet now by grace brought nigh to Him,

   Through faith in Jesus' blood.

 

 3 

So nigh, so very nigh to God,

   I cannot nearer be;

For in the person of His Son

   I am as near as He.

 

 4 

So dear, so very dear to God,

   More dear I cannot be;

The love wherewith He loves the Son,

   Such is His love to me.

 

 5 

Why should I ever anxious be,

   Since such a God is mine?

He watches o'er me night and day,

   And tells me "Mine is thine."
