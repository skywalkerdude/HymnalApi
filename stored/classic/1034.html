Hymns, #1034

Gospel - Persuasion

Eng/Kor:1034  Chin:709  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:1034  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Philip P. Bliss
Music:Philip P. Bliss
Meter:11.11.11.7. with chorus.
Guitar:No Capo, C Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

1029 1030 1031 1032 1033 More 1035 1036 1037 1038 1039

Stanzas:  1   2   3 

 

 1 

"Whosoever heareth!" shout, shout the sound!

Send the blessed tidings all the world around;

Spread the joyful news wherever man is found:

   "Whosoever will may come!"

 

"Whosoever will! Whosoever will!"

Send the proclamation over vale and hill;

'Tis a loving Father calls the wand'rer home:

   "Whosoever will may come!"

 

 2 

"Whosoever cometh" need not delay,

Now the door is open, enter while you may;

Jesus is the true, the only Living Way:

   "Whosoever will may come!"

 

"Whosoever will! Whosoever will!"

Send the proclamation over vale and hill;

'Tis a loving Father calls the wand'rer home:

   "Whosoever will may come!"

 

 3 

"Whosoever will!" the promise is secure;

"Whosoever will," forever must endure;

"Whosoever will!" 'tis life forevermore;

   "Whosoever will may come."

 

"Whosoever will! Whosoever will!"

Send the proclamation over vale and hill;

'Tis a loving Father calls the wand'rer home:

   "Whosoever will may come!"
