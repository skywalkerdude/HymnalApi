Hymns, #545

Experience of Christ - Love for Him

Eng/Kor:545  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Robert Murray McCheyne
Music:(1) Richard Redhead
(2) Dimitri Bortnianski
Meter:7.7.7.7.7.7.
Guitar:Capo 1, Eb Major [Sheet Music]

Hymn using same tune: Tune 2: #637

540 541 542 543 544 More 546 547 548 549 550

Stanzas:  1   2   3 

 

 1 

When this passing world is done,

When has sunk yon glaring sun,

When we stand with Christ on high,

Looking o'er life's history;

Then, Lord, shall I fully know,

Not till then, how much I owe.

 

 2 

When I stand before the throne

Dressed in beauty not my own,

When I see Thee as Thou art,

Love Thee with unsinning heart;

Then, Lord, shall I fully know,

Not till then, how much I owe.

 

 3 

E'en on earth, as through a glass

Darkly, let Thy glory pass;

Make forgiveness feel so sweet;

Make Thy Spirit's help so meet:

E'en on earth, Lord, make me know

Something of how much I owe.
