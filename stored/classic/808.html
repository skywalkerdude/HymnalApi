Hymns, #808

Study of the Word - Seeking for the Word

Eng/Kor:808  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:808  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Frances R. Havergal
Music:Lowell Mason
Meter:8.7.8.7.7.7.
Guitar:Capo 3, F Major [Sheet Music]

Hymn using same tune: #144

803 804 805 806 807 More 809 810 811 812 813

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4   5 

 

 1 

Master, speak! Thy servant heareth,

   Waiting for Thy gracious word,

Longing for Thy voice that cheereth,

   Master, let it now be heard.

I am list'ning, Lord, for Thee;

What hast Thou to say to me?

 

 2 

Often through my heart is pealing

   Many another voice than Thine,

Many an unwilled echo stealing

   From the walls of this Thy shrine.

Let Thy longed-for accents fall;

Master, speak! and silence all.

 

 3 

Master speak! though least and lowest,

   Let me not unheard depart;

Master, speak! for oh, Thou knowest

   All the yearning of my heart.

Knowest all its truest need;

Speak! and make me blest indeed.

 

 4 

Master speak! and make me ready,

   When Thy voice is truly heard,

With obedience glad and steady,

   Still to follow every word,

I am listening, Lord, for Thee:

Master, speak, oh, speak to me!

 

 5 

Speak to me by name, O Master,

   Let me know it is to me;

Speak, that I may follow faster,

   With a step more firm and free,

Where the Shepherd leads the flock,

In the shadow of the Rock!
