Hymns, #619

Glorying in the Cross - Towering over All

Eng/Kor:619  Chin:455  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:619  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:John Bowring
Music:Ithamar Conkey
Meter:8.7.8.7.
Guitar:Capo 3, Bb Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

614 615 616 617 618 More 620 621 622 623 624

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4 

 

 1 

In the cross of Christ I glory,

   Tow'ring o'er the wrecks of time;

All the light of sacred story

   Gather round its head sublime.

 

 2 

When the woes of life o'ertake me,

   Hopes deceive, and fears annoy,

Never shall the cross forsake me,

   Lo! It glows with peace and joy.

 

 3 

When the sun of bliss is beaming

   Light and love upon my way,

From the cross the radiance streaming

   Adds more luster to the day.

 

 4 

Bane and blessing, pain and pleasure,

   By the cross are sanctified;

Peace is there that knows no measure,

   Joys that through all time abide.
