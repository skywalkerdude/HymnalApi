Praise of the Lord - Remembrance of Him

Eng/Kor:231  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Charles H. Spurgeon
Music:Alfred P. Gibbs
Meter:8.8.8.8.
Guitar:No Capo, G Major [Sheet Music]

Hymn using same tune: #734

226 227 228 229 230 More 232 233 234 235 236

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4 

 

 1 

Amidst us our Beloved stands,

And bids us view His piercèd hands;

Points to the wounded feet and side,

Blest emblems of the Crucified.

 

 2 

What food luxurious loads the board

When, at His table, sits the Lord!

The wine how rich, the bread how sweet,

When Jesus deigns the guests to meet!

 

 3 

If now, with eyes defiled and dim,

We see the signs, but see not Him;

Oh, may His love the scales displace,

And bid us see Him face to face!

 

 4 

Thou glorious Bridegroom of our hearts,

Thy present smile Thy grace imparts!

Oh, lift the veil, if veil there be,

Let every saint Thy glory see!
