Hymns, #719

Comfort in Trials - By Peace in the Lord

Eng/Kor:719  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Frances R. Havergal
Music:James Mountain
Meter:6.5.6.5.D.
Guitar:Capo 3, F Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

714 715 716 717 718 More 720 721 722 723 724

Stanzas:  1   2   3 

 

 1 

Like a river, glorious

   Is God's perfect peace,

Over all victorious

   In its bright increase;

Perfect, yet it floweth

   Fuller every day,

Perfect, yet it groweth

   Deeper all the way.

 

Stayed upon Christ Jesus,

Hearts are fully blest;

Finding, as He promised,

Perfect peace and rest.

 

 2 

Hidden in the hollow

   Of His blessed hand,

Never foe can follow,

   Never traitor stand;

Not a surge of worry,

   Not a shade of care,

Not a blast of hurry

   Touch the spirit there.

 

Stayed upon Christ Jesus,

Hearts are fully blest;

Finding, as He promised,

Perfect peace and rest.

 

 3 

Every joy or trial

   Falleth from above,

Traced upon our dial

   By the Sun of Love.

We must trust Him fully

   All for us to do;

They who trust Him wholly

   Find Him wholly true.

 

Stayed upon Christ Jesus,

Hearts are fully blest;

Finding, as He promised,

Perfect peace and rest.
