Hymns, #999

Gospel - Redemption

Eng/Kor:999  Chin:681  Span:464  Rus:163  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Isaac Watts, chorus by Ralph E. Hudson
Music:Ralph E. Hudson
Meter:8.6.8.6. with chorus.
Guitar:Capo 2, E Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

994 995 996 997 998 More 1000 1001 1002 1003 1004

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4   5 

 

 1 

Alas, and did my Savior bleed?

   And did my Sovereign die?

Would He devote that sacred head

   For such a worm as I?

 

At the cross, at the cross where

   I first saw the light,

And the burden of my heart rolled away,

   It was there by faith I received my sight,

And now I am happy all the day!

 

 2 

Was it for sins that I had done

   He groaned upon the tree?

Amazing pity! Grace unknown!

   And love beyond degree!

 

At the cross, at the cross where

   I first saw the light,

And the burden of my heart rolled away,

   It was there by faith I received my sight,

And now I am happy all the day!

 

 3 

Well might the sun in darkness hide,

   And shut His glories in,

When Christ, the mighty Maker, died

   For man, His creature's sin.

 

At the cross, at the cross where

   I first saw the light,

And the burden of my heart rolled away,

   It was there by faith I received my sight,

And now I am happy all the day!

 

 4 

Thus might I hide my blushing face

   While His dear cross appears.

Dissolve my heart in thankfulness,

   And melt mine eyes to tears.

 

At the cross, at the cross where

   I first saw the light,

And the burden of my heart rolled away,

   It was there by faith I received my sight,

And now I am happy all the day!

 

 5 

But drops of grief can ne'er repay

   The debt of love I owe;

Here, Lord, I give myself away,

   'Tis all that I can do.

 

At the cross, at the cross where

   I first saw the light,

And the burden of my heart rolled away,

   It was there by faith I received my sight,

And now I am happy all the day!
