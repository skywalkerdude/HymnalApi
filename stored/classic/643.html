Hymns, #643

Encouragement - For Fellowship with the Lord

Eng/Kor:643  Chin:477  Span:-  Rus:466  Tag:643  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:William D. Longstaff
Music:George C. Stebbins
Meter:6.5.6.5.D.
Guitar:Capo 3, F Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

638 639 640 641 642 More 644 645 646 647 648

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4 

 

 1 

Take time to behold Him,

   Speak oft with thy Lord,

Abide in Him always,

   And feed on His Word.

Wait thou in His presence,

   Submissive and meek,

Forgetting in nothing

   His blessing to seek.

 

 2 

Take time to behold Him,

   The world rushes on;

Spend much time in secret

   With Jesus alone.

By looking to Jesus

   Like Him thou shalt be;

Thy friends, in thy conduct,

   His likeness shall see.

 

 3 

Take time to behold Him,

   Let Him be thy guide;

And run not before Him

   Whatever betide;

In joy or in sorrow

   Still follow thy Lord,

And, looking to Jesus,

   Still trust in His Word.

 

 4 

Take time to behold Him,

   Be calm in thy soul,

Each thought and each temper

   Beneath His control.

Thus led by His Spirit

   To fountains of love,

Thou then shalt be fitted

   His mercy to prove.
