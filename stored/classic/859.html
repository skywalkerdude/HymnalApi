Hymns, #859

The Church - Her Fellowship

Eng/Kor:859  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:B. Beddome
Music:From Swedish Melodier till Trossanger
Meter:6.6.8.6.
Guitar:No Capo, G Major [Sheet Music]

Hymn using same tune: #615 (Tune 2)

854 855 856 857 858 More 860 861 862 863 864

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4 

 

 1 

   Let party names no more

   The Christian world o'erspread;

Gentile and Jew, and bond and free,

   Are one in Christ, their Head.

 

 2 

   Among the saints on earth

   Let mutual love be found;

Heirs of the same inheritance,

   With mutual blessings crowned.

 

 3 

   Let envy, child of hell!

   Be banished far away:

Those in sweet fellowship should dwell,

   Who the same Lord obey.

 

 4 

   Thus will the Church below

   God's purpose ever prove;

Where streams of endless pleasure flow,

   And every heart is love.
