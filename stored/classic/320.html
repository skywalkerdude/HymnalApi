Hymns, #320

Assurance and Joy of Salvation - Saved by Grace

Eng/Kor:320  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Charles H. Gabriel
Music:Charles H. Gabriel
Meter:8.8.8.6. with chorus.
Guitar:Capo 3, F Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

315 316 317 318 319 More 321 322 323 324 325

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4 

 

 1 

In loving-kindness Jesus came

   My soul in mercy to reclaim,

And from the depths of sin and shame

   Through grace He lifted me.

 

From sinking sand He lifted me,

With tender hand He lifted me,

From shades of night to plains of light,

Oh, praise His name, He lifted me!

 

 2 

He called me long before I heard,

   Before my sinful heart was stirred,

But when I took Him at His word,

   Forgiv'n, He lifted me.

 

From sinking sand He lifted me,

With tender hand He lifted me,

From shades of night to plains of light,

Oh, praise His name, He lifted me!

 

 3 

His brow was pierced with many a thorn,

   His hands by cruel nails were torn,

When from my guilt and grief, forlorn,

   In love He lifted me.

 

From sinking sand He lifted me,

With tender hand He lifted me,

From shades of night to plains of light,

Oh, praise His name, He lifted me!

 

 4 

Now on a higher plane I dwell,

   And with my soul I know 'tis well;

Yet how or why, I cannot tell,

   He should have lifted me.

 

From sinking sand He lifted me,

With tender hand He lifted me,

From shades of night to plains of light,

Oh, praise His name, He lifted me!
