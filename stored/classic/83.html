Hymns, #83

Praise of the Lord - His Incarnation

Eng/Kor:83  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:83  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:John Cawood
Music:From Gotha Cantional
Meter:8.7.8.7.
Guitar:Capo 3, F Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

78 79 80 81 82 More 84 85 86 87 88

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4   5   6 

 

 1 

Hark! What mean those holy voices

   Sweetly sounding in the skies?

Lo! th' angelic host rejoices,

   Loudest hallelujahs rise.

 

 2 

Listen to the wondrous story

   Which they chant in hymns of joy:

"Glory in the highest, glory!

   Glory be to God most high!

 

 3 

"Peace on earth, good-will from heaven,

   Reaching far as man is found;

Souls redeemed and sins forgiven,

   Loud our golden harps shall sound.

 

 4 

"Christ is born! The great Anointed!

   Heaven and earth His glory sing;

Oh, receive whom God appointed

   For your Prophet, Priest, and King!

 

 5 

"Hasten, mortals, to adore Him

   Learn His name and taste His joy,

Till one day ye sing before Him

   Glory be to God most high! "

 

 6 

Let us learn the wondrous story

   Of our great Redeemer's birth;

Spread the brightness of His glory,

   Till it cover all the earth.
