The Way of the Cross - Gain by Loss

Eng/Kor:625  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:625  Por:-

Lyrics:William Cowper
Music:Wolfgang A. Mozart
Meter:7.7.7.7.
Guitar:Capo 3, F Major [Sheet Music]

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4 

 

 1 

'Tis my happiness below

Not to live without the cross;

But the Savior's pow'r to know

Sanctifying every loss.

 

 2 

Trials must and will befall;

But with humble faith to see

Love inscribed upon them all —

This is happiness to me.

 

 3 

Did I meet no trials here,

No chastisement by the way,

Might I not with reason fear

I should prove a castaway?

 

 4 

Trials make the promise sweet;

Trials give new life to prayer;

Bring me to my Savior's feet,

Lay me low and keep me there.
