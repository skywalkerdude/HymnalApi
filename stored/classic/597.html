Hymns, #597

Experience of Christ - General

Eng/Kor:597  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:597  Por:307

Lyrics:Charles Wesley
Music:Arr. from George Frederick Handel
Meter:8.6.8.6.
Guitar:No Capo, C Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

592 593 594 595 596 More 598 599 600 601 602

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4   5 

 

 1 

I know that my Redeemer lives

   And ever prays for me;

A token of His love He gives,

   A pledge of liberty.

 

 2 

I know that my Redeemer lives,

   A quick'ning Spirit He;

I know eternal life He gives —

   Amazing grace — to me.

 

 3 

I find Him lifting up my head;

   He brings salvation near;

His presence make me free indeed,

   And He will soon appear.

 

 4 

He wills that I should holy be:

   What can withstand His will?

The counsel of His grace in me

   He surely shall fulfill.

 

 5 

Jesus, I hang upon Thy word:

   I steadfastly believe

Thou wilt return and claim me, Lord,

   And to Thyself receive.
