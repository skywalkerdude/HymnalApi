Hymns, #330

Assurance and Joy of Salvation - Secured by Divine Provisions

Eng/Kor:330  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Lidie H. Edmunds
Music:Norse air, arr. by William J. Kirkpatrick
Meter:8.6.8.6.D.
Guitar:No Capo, G Major [Sheet Music]

Hymn using same tune: #1188

325 326 327 328 329 More 331 332 333 334 335

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4 

 

 1 

My faith has found a resting place,

   Not in device nor creed;

I trust the Ever-living One,

   His wounds for me shall plead.

 

I need no other argument,

   I need no other plea;

It is enough that Jesus died,

   And that He died for me.

 

 2 

Enough for me that Jesus saves,

   This ends my fear and doubt;

A sinful soul I come to Him,

   He'll never cast me out.

 

I need no other argument,

   I need no other plea;

It is enough that Jesus died,

   And that He died for me.

 

 3 

My heart is leaning on the Word,

   The written Word of God,

Salvation by my Savior's name,

   Salvation through His blood.

 

I need no other argument,

   I need no other plea;

It is enough that Jesus died,

   And that He died for me.

 

 4 

My great Physician heals the sick,

   The lost He came to save;

For me His precious blood He shed,

   For me His life He gave.

 

I need no other argument,

   I need no other plea;

It is enough that Jesus died,

   And that He died for me.
