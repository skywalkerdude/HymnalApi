Hymns, #516

Experience of Christ - As Everything

Eng/Kor:516  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:251

Lyrics:Oliver Wendell Holmes
Music:John L. Hatton
Meter:8.8.8.8.
Guitar:No Capo, D Major [Sheet Music]

Hymns using same tune:

#145, #184, #211, #492, #494, #835, #1184

511 512 513 514 515 More 517 518 519 520 521

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4   5 

 

 1 

Lord of all being, throned afar,

Thy glory flames from sun and star;

Center and light of every sphere,

Yet to each loving heart how near.

 

 2 

Sun of our life, Thy quickening ray

Sheds on our path the glow of day;

Star of our hope, Thy softened light

Cheers the long watches of the night.

 

 3 

Our midnight is Thy smile withdrawn,

Our noontide is Thy gracious dawn,

Our rainbow arch Thy mercy's sign;

All, save the clouds of sin, are Thine.

 

 4 

Lord of all life, below, above,

Whose light is truth, whose warmth is love,

Before Thy ever-blazing throne

We ask no luster of our own.

 

 5 

Grant us Thy truth to make us free,

And kindling hearts that burn for Thee,

Till all Thy living altars claim

One holy light, one heavenly flame.
