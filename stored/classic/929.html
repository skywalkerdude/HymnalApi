Hymns, #929

Preaching of the Gospel - A Channel of Blessing

Eng/Kor:929  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:929  Fra:929  Por:-

Lyrics:Ira B. Wilson
Music:George S. Schuler
Meter:10.7.10.7. with chorus.
Guitar:Capo 3, Bb Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

924 925 926 927 928 More 930 931 932 933 934

Stanzas:  1   2   3 

 

 1 

Out in the highways and byways of life,

   Many are weary and sad;

Carry the sunshine where darkness is rife,

   Making the sorrowing glad.

 

Make me a blessing, make me a blessing,

Out of my life may Jesus shine;

Make me a blessing, O Savior, I pray,

Make me a blessing to someone today.

 

 2 

Tell the sweet story of Christ and His love,

   Tell of His pow'r to forgive;

Others will trust Him if only you prove

   True, every moment you live.

 

Make me a blessing, make me a blessing,

Out of my life may Jesus shine;

Make me a blessing, O Savior, I pray,

Make me a blessing to someone today.

 

 3 

Give as 'twas given to you in your need,

   Love as the Master loved you;

Be to the helpless a helper indeed,

   Unto your mission be true.

 

Make me a blessing, make me a blessing,

Out of my life may Jesus shine;

Make me a blessing, O Savior, I pray,

Make me a blessing to someone today.
