Hymns, #684

Comfort in Trials - By the Lord's Mercy-Seat

Eng/Kor:684  Chin:502  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:684  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Thomas Moore, altered by Thomas Hastings
Music:Samuel Webbe
Meter:11.10.11.10.
Guitar:No Capo, C Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

679 680 681 682 683 More 685 686 687 688 689

Stanzas:  1   2   3 

 

 1 

Come, ye disconsolate, where'er ye languish;

Come to the mercy-seat, fervently kneel;

Here bring your wounded hearts, here tell your anguish,

Earth has no sorrow that heaven cannot heal.

 

 2 

Joy of the comfortless, light of the straying,

Hope of the penitent, fadeless and pure;

Here speaks the Comforter, tenderly saying —

Earth has no sorrow that heaven cannot cure.

 

 3 

Here see the Bread of Life; see waters flowing

Forth from the throne of God, pure from above;

Come to the feast of love; come, ever knowing

Earth has no sorrow but heaven can remove.
