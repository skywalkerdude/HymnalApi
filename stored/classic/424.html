Longings - For Rest

Eng/Kor:424  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:195

Lyrics:Charles Wesley
Music:(1) William H. Havergal
(2) Handley C. G. Moule
Meter:8.6.8.6.
Guitar:Capo 1, Ab Major [Sheet Music]

Hymns using same tune:

Tune 2: #414, #658

419 420 421 422 423 More 425 426 427 428 429

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4   5 

 

 1 

Lord, I believe a rest remains

   To all Thy people known;

A rest where pure enjoyment reigns,

   And Thou art loved alone.

 

 2 

A rest, where all our soul's desire

   Is fixed on things above;

Where fear and sin and grief expire,

   Cast out by perfect love.

 

 3 

Oh, that I now the rest might know,

   Believe, and enter in;

Now, Savior, now the power bestow,

   And let me cease from sin.

 

 4 

Remove this hardness from my heart,

   This unbelief remove;

To me the rest of faith impart,

   The Sabbath of Thy love.

 

 5 

I would be Thine, Thou know'st I would,

   And have Thee all my own;

Thee, O my all-sufficient Good.

   I want, and Thee alone.
