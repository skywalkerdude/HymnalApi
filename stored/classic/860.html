Hymns, #860

The Church - Her Fellowship

Eng/Kor:860  Chin:621  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:860  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:John Fawcett
Music:From Hans G. Nageli, arr. by Lowell Mason
Meter:6.6.8.6.
Guitar:Capo 3, F Major [Sheet Music]

Hymn using same tune: #615 (Tune 1)

855 856 857 858 859 More 861 862 863 864 865

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4   5 

 

 1 

   Blest be the tie that binds

   Our hearts in Christian love;

The fellowship our spirit finds

   Is like to that above.

 

 2 

   Before our Father's throne,

   We pour our ardent prayers;

Our fears, our hopes, our aims are one —

   Our comforts and our cares.

 

 3 

   We share our mutual woes;

   Our mutual burdens bear;

And often of each other flows

   The sympathizing tear.

 

 4 

   When we asunder part,

   It gives us inward pain;

But we shall still be joined in heart,

   And hope to meet again.

 

 5 

   From sorrow, toil, and pain,

   And sin we shall be free;

And perfect love and oneness reign

   Through all eternity.
