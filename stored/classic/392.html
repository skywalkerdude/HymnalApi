Longings - For Christ's Leading

Eng/Kor:392  Chin:507  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:392  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Edward Hopper
Music:John E. Gould
Meter:7.7.7.7.7.7.
Guitar:Capo 1, Ab Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

Stanzas:  1   2   3 

 

 1 

Jesus, Savior, pilot me

Over life's tempestuous sea;

Unknown waves before me roll,

Hiding rock and treach'rous shoal;

Chart and compass come from Thee;

   Jesus, Savior, pilot me.

 

 2 

As a mother stills her child,

Thou canst hush the ocean wild;

Boist'rous waves obey Thy will

When Thou say'st to them, "Be still."

Wondrous Sov'reign of the sea,

   Jesus, Savior, pilot me.

 

 3 

Though death's valley I may pass,

Still Thy grace will fear surpass;

In Thy presence I will rest,

And, while leaning on Thy breast,

I will hear Thee say to me,

   "Fear not, I will pilot thee."
