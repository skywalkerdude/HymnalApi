Hymns, #685

Comfort in Trials - By the Lord's Presence

Eng/Kor:685  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Unknown
Music:A. Legge
Meter:5.4.5.4.D.
Guitar:No Capo, D Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

680 681 682 683 684 More 686 687 688 689 690

Stanzas:  1   2   3 

 

 1 

Lord, Thou art with me!

   Whom shall I fear?

Who then can harm me

   While Thou art near?

Under Thy shadow

   Is my retreat;

There to my taste,

   Lord, Thy fruit is sweet.

 

 2 

Lord, Thou art with me,

   And Thou dost give

Strength for the journey,

   Grace now to live.

Mercies each morning

   Thou dost renew;

And for my prospect,

   Thy rest in view.

 

 3 

Lord, Thou art with me;

   Onward I move;

Each day I'm proving

   More of Thy love.

Now Thou art training,

   Setting me free

From all that hinders,

   To dwell with Thee.
