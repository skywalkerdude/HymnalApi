Hymns, #727

Comfort in Trials - By Gain From Suffering

Eng/Kor:727  Chin:528  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Frances R. Havergal
Music:Ira D. Sankey
Meter:5.4.5.4.D.
Guitar:No Capo, D Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

722 723 724 725 726 More 728 729 730 731 732

Stanzas:  1   2   3 

 

 1 

Light after darkness,

   Gain after loss,

Strength after weakness,

   Crown after cross;

Sweet after bitter,

   Hope after fears,

Home after wand'ring,

   Praise after tears.

 

 2 

Sheaves after sowing,

   Sun after rain,

Sight after mystery,

   Peace after pain;

Joy after sorrow,

   Calm after blast,

Rest after weariness,

   Sweet rest at last.

 

 3 

Near after distant,

   Gleam after gloom,

Love after loneliness,

   Life after tomb;

After long agony,

   Rapture of bliss,

Right was the pathway,

   Leading to this.
