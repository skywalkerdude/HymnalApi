Hymns, #682

Comfort in Trials - By the Lord's Hands

Eng/Kor:682  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Unknown
Music:John Richardson
Meter:8.6.8.6.
Guitar:Capo 1, Eb Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

677 678 679 680 681 More 683 684 685 686 687

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4   5 

 

 1 

When I survey life's varied scene,

   Amid the darkest hours

Sweet rays of comfort shine between

   And thorns are mixed with flowers.

 

 2 

Lord, teach me to adore Thy hand,

   From whence my comforts flow,

And let me in this desert land

   A glimpse of glory know.

 

 3 

Father, whate'er of earthly bliss

   Thy sovereign hand denies,

Accepted at Thy throne of grace,

   Let this petition rise:

 

 4 

Give me a calm, a thankful heart,

   From every murmur free;

The blessings of Thy grace impart,

   And let me live to Thee.

 

 5 

Let the sweet hope that Thou art mine

   My life and death attend,

Thy presence through my journey shine,

   And crown my journey's end.
