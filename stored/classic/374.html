Longings - For Fellowship with Christ

Eng/Kor:374  Chin:407  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:374  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:John Keble
Music:From Katholisches Gesangbuch, 1774
Meter:8.8.8.8.
Guitar:Capo 3, F Major [Sheet Music]

Hymns using same tune:

#610, #742, #814, #979

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4 

 

 1 

Sun of my soul, Thou Savior dear,

It is not night if Thou be near;

Oh, may no earthborn cloud arise

To hide Thee from Thy servant's eyes.

 

 2 

When the soft dews of kindly sleep

My wearied eyelids gently steep,

Be my last thought how sweet to rest

Forever on my Savior's breast.

 

 3 

Abide with me from morn till eve,

For without Thee I cannot live;

Abide with me when night is nigh,

For without Thee I dare not die.

 

 4 

Come near, and bless us when we wake,

Ere through the world our way we take;

Till in the ocean of Thy love

We lose ourselves in Thee above.
