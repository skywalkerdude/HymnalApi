Hymns, #584

Experience of Christ - Following Him

Eng/Kor:584  Chin:-  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Jessie B. Pounds
Music:Daniel B. Towner
Meter:11.11.11.11. with chorus.
Guitar:No Capo, C Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

579 580 581 582 583 More 585 586 587 588 589

Stanzas:  1   2   3 

 

 1 

Anywhere with Jesus I can safely go,

Anywhere He leads me in this world below.

Anywhere without Him, dearest joys would fade;

Anywhere with Jesus I am not afraid.

 

Anywhere! Anywhere! Fear I cannot know.

Anywhere with Jesus I can safely go.

 

 2 

Anywhere with Jesus I am not alone,

Other friends may fail me, He is still my own.

Though His hand may lead me over dreary ways,

Anywhere with Jesus is a house of praise.

 

Anywhere! Anywhere! Fear I cannot know.

Anywhere with Jesus I can safely go.

 

 3 

Anywhere with Jesus I can go to sleep,

When the darkling shadows round about me creep;

Knowing I shall waken never more to roam,

Anywhere with Jesus will be home, sweet home.

 

Anywhere! Anywhere! Fear I cannot know.

Anywhere with Jesus I can safely go.
