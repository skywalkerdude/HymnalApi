Hymns, #310

Assurance and Joy of Salvation - Freed by the Lord

Eng/Kor:310  Chin:246  Span:153  Rus:65  Tag:310  Fra:310  Por:173

Lyrics:Haldor Lillenas
Music:Alfred Judson
Meter:10.9.10.9.D.
Guitar:No Capo, D Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

305 306 307 308 309 More 311 312 313 314 315

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4 

 

 1 

Once I was bound by sin's galling fetters,

Chained like a slave I struggled in vain;

But I received a glorious freedom,

When Jesus broke my fetters in twain.

 

Glorious freedom, wonderful freedom,

No more in chains of sin I repine!

Jesus the glorious Emancipator,

Now and forever He shall be mine.

 

 2 

Freedom from all the carnal affections,

Freedom from envy, hatred and strife;

Freedom from vain and worldly ambitions,

Freedom from all that saddened my life.

 

Glorious freedom, wonderful freedom,

No more in chains of sin I repine!

Jesus the glorious Emancipator,

Now and forever He shall be mine.

 

 3 

Freedom from pride and all sinful follies,

Freedom from love and glitter of gold;

Freedom from evil temper and anger,

Glorious freedom, rapture untold.

 

Glorious freedom, wonderful freedom,

No more in chains of sin I repine!

Jesus the glorious Emancipator,

Now and forever He shall be mine.

 

 4 

Freedom from fear with all of its torments,

Freedom from care with all of its pain;

Freedom in Christ my blessed Redeemer,

He who has rent my fetters in twain.

 

Glorious freedom, wonderful freedom,

No more in chains of sin I repine!

Jesus the glorious Emancipator,

Now and forever He shall be mine.
