Hymns, #348

Assurance and Joy of Salvation - General

Eng/Kor:348  Chin:256  Span:165  Rus:255  Tag:348  Fra:-  Por:181

Lyrics:C. F. Butler
Music:James M. Black
Meter:8.8.8.8.D.
Guitar:No Capo, G Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

343 344 345 346 347 More 349 350 351 352 353

Stanzas:  1   2   3 

 

 1 

Since Christ my soul from sin set free,

This life has been a joy to me;

And 'mid earth's sorrows and its woe,

'Tis joy my Jesus here to know.

 

O hallelujah, yes, 'tis joy!

For it is Christ that I enjoy!

On land or sea, what matters where?

Where Jesus is, my joy is there.

 

 2 

The earth was once a bitter place

Till Jesus showed His smiling face;

Now joy's begun within my soul,

'Twill last while endless ages roll.

 

O hallelujah, yes, 'tis joy!

For it is Christ that I enjoy!

On land or sea, what matters where?

Where Jesus is, my joy is there.

 

 3 

What matters where on earth we dwell?

On mountain top, or in the dell,

In cottage, or a mansion fair,

Where Jesus is, my joy is there.

 

O hallelujah, yes, 'tis joy!

For it is Christ that I enjoy!

On land or sea, what matters where?

Where Jesus is, my joy is there.
