Hymns, #89

Praise of the Lord - His Life

Eng/Kor:89  Chin:78[*]  Span:-  Rus:-  Tag:89  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Edward Denny
Music:Samuel Arnold
Meter:8.6.8.6.
Guitar:No Capo, D Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

84 85 86 87 88 More 90 91 92 93 94

Stanzas:  1   2   3 

 

 1 

What grace, O Lord, and beauty shone

   Around Thy steps below!

What patient love was seen in all

   Thy life and death of woe!

 

 2 

Forever on Thy burdened heart

   A weight of sorrow hung,

Yet no ungentle, murm'ring word

   Escaped Thy silent tongue.

 

 3 

Thy foes did hate, despise, revile,

   Thy friends unfaithful prove;

Unwearied in forgiveness still

   Thy heart could only love!
