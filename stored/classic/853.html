Hymns, #853

The Church - Her Attraction

Eng/Kor:853  Chin:s753  Span:366  Rus:430  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Timothy Dwight
Music:Aaron Williams
Meter:6.6.8.6.
Guitar:Capo 3, F Major [Sheet Music]

Hymn using same tune: #1306

848 849 850 851 852 More 854 855 856 857 858

Stanzas:  1   2   3   4   5 

 

 1 

   I love Thy kingdom, Lord,

   The house of Thine abode,

The Church our blest Redeemer bought

   With His own precious blood.

 

 2 

   I love the Church, O God!

   Her walls before Thee stand,

Dear as the apple of Thine eye

   And graven on Thy hand.

 

 3 

   For her my tears shall fall,

   For her my prayers ascend;

To her my cares and toils be given

   Till toils and cares shall end.

 

 4 

   Beyond my highest joy

   I prize her heavenly ways,

Her sweet communion, solemn vows,

   Her hymns of love and praise.

 

 5 

   Sure as Thy truth shall last,

   To Zion shall be given

The brightest glories earth can yield,

   And brighter bliss of heaven.
