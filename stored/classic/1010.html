Hymns, #1010

Gospel - The Blood

Eng/Kor:1010  Chin:s854  Span:473  Rus:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:-

Lyrics:Mrs. Phoebe Palmer
Music:Mrs. Joseph F. Knapp
Meter:8.6.8.6. with chorus.
Guitar:Capo 1, Eb Major [Sheet Music]

This hymn has a unique tune.

1005 1006 1007 1008 1009 More 1011 1012 1013 1014 1015

Stanzas:  1   2   3 

 

 1 

O now I see the cleansing wave!

   The fountain deep and wide;

Jesus, my Lord, mighty to save,

   Points to His wounded side.

 

The cleansing stream I see, I see;

I plunge, and O it cleanseth me!

O praise the Lord it cleanseth me!

It cleanseth me, yes, cleanseth me.

 

 2 

I see the dear Redeemer raised,

   I hear the speaking blood;

It speaks! My spirit is amazed!

   And quickened by its flood.

 

The cleansing stream I see, I see;

I plunge, and O it cleanseth me!

O praise the Lord it cleanseth me!

It cleanseth me, yes, cleanseth me.

 

 3 

I rise to walk in God's own light

   Above the world and sin,

With heart renewed and garments white

   And Christ enthroned within.

 

The cleansing stream I see, I see;

I plunge, and O it cleanseth me!

O praise the Lord it cleanseth me!

It cleanseth me, yes, cleanseth me.
